SO BASICALLY TO ADD LINES TYPE IN TEXTBOX OR TEXTAREA (SEPERATE BY NEWLINE)
TO SHOW NEW LINES CLICK SHOW NEW LINE
CLICK SUBTRACT LINE TO HIDE LAST LINE
CLICK SUBTRACT ALL LINES TO HIDE ALL LINES
WHEN LINE IS SHOWN:
  CLICK REMOVE LINE TO GET RID OF IT PERMANENTLY
  CLICK EDIT LINE TO EDIT LINE

thats it!
